# 15-keypoint format

We use the first 15 keypoints defined in BODY_25.

1 Neck
2 R_shoulder
3 R_elbow
4 R_wrist
5 L_shoulder
6 L_elbow
7 L_wrist
8 Mid_hip
9 R_hip
10 R_knee
11 R_ankle
12 L_hip
13 L_knee
14 L_ankle
